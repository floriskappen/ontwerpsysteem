# States

States follow a lifecycle metaphor:
1. **Fallow (Empty)**: Renders tilled but empty ground rules (parallel stripes).
2. **Germinating (Loading)**: Renders a growing spiral seed head filling in golden angle order.
3. **Ripe (Complete)**: Swells and deepens color (no green ticks).
4. **Rising (Progress)**: A vessel filling with water from the bottom edge in stepped ticks.
