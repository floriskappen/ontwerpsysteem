# Material

Material rules govern the tactile presence:
1. **Paper Tooth**: Subtle multiplied noise texture overlay gives the page a warm, physical paper feel.
2. **Ink and Pigment**: Multiplied organic color blots that deepen subtractively where they overlay (simulating liquid ink bleed).
3. **Phyllotaxis Dividers**: Structural markings follow Vogel's sunflower spiral seed model to convey grown order.
